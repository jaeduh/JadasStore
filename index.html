Sneaker "Culture"

Sneakers have evolved from being a tool in athletics to an entire culture, movement, and lifestyle. Athletes like Michael Jordan and brands like Nike took sneakers and stylized them in ways people could never forget. It was not until Michael Jordan partnered with Nike and created his own silhouette that sneakers went from athletic use to another way of self expression.
